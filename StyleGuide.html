Style guide and inspiration

This is guide on on the style the website is heading. I've included external websites that have given me inspiration.
The website will be a retro 90's themed website. I have chosen this theme as I think it complements my charactures choice. the 90's theme highlights the outof date views and age of my characture. It gives the illusion that this website has been up for many years and the characture has not changed it with the times, as he himself has not changed. I think it also gives the effect that the characture is weary of new technologies as hes not keeping up with cutting edge tech.
